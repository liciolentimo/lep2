Practice Areas

Commercial Law

Preparation of debentures and chattel mortgages.Formation and incorporation of companies and Trusts. Preparation of transfers and business mergers.

Property

Conveyancing. Preparation and registration of charges and mortgages. Preparation of sale agreements and property transfers. Formation of trust, probate and administration, mergers and takeovers.

Intellectual Property

Preparation, processing and registration of copyright and trade marks.

Civil Litigation

Court appearing, defending and suing in civil matters on behalf of individual and corporate clients. Judicial review and constitutional references.

Alternative Dispute Resolution and Arbitration

We undertake arbitration proceedings and have in the past handled High Profile cases. Indeed we encourage our clients to use this more expeditious mode of dispute settlement.

Debt Collection

This involves acting for our clients by demanding money due to them, filing suits and defending suits against them. This is for both corporate and individual clients.

Recoveries

Realization of securities through issuance of legal notices, sale of charged or mortgaged property by public auction or private treaty.

Criminal Litigation

We undertake defence and advice in all type of criminal proceedings.

Consultancy Services

Our law firm offers legal consultancy services of various issues to Government Institutions, Corporates individuals and Development Agencies including: Legal Audit, Copany Law Services, Legislative Drafting of New Laws and amendment of existing ones.
